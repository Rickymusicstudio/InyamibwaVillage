Ikaze

Murakaza neza k'urubuga rw'Umudugudu w'Inyamibwa. Uru rubuga rugenewe ihuriro ry’abaturage bose b’umudugudu wacu, aho mushobora kubona amakuru y'umudugudu, ubufasha butandukanye, ndetse no gutanga ibitekerezo byubaka umudugudu wacu.

  • 📌 Amakuru y’ingirakamaro yerekeye ibikorwa n’iterambere ry’umudugudu
  • 🛠️ Ubufasha mu byerekeye imibereho myiza, umutekano, n’imibano
  • 💬 Uburyo bwo gutanga ibibazo, ibitekerezo cyangwa ibitekerezo byubaka
  • 🔗 Serivisi zihuza abaturage n’ubuyobozi

Dukomeze twubake umudugudu utekanye kandi uhamye, dutahiriza umugozi umwe.

Murakaza neza, kandi murisanga!
